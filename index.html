About Me


I am Nick Schoess, I grew up in Stacy, MN which is about an hour north of the Twin Cities. I enjoy all things creative! I have been playing music, doing photography, videography, skateboarding, skiing, etc. for as long as I can remember. Web development is something I have gotten into later in life, yet is extremely gratifying for me. I love bringing all of the puzzle pieces together to make something that is not only beautiful, but functional as well.

In my career, I love new challenges and opportunities to learn. Growing is a large part of what I feel is a joyful and fulfilling life. I look forward to finding new problems to solve, as well as new people to collaborate and work with. I truly believe that coding and dev work will give me pleanty of that during my career.
